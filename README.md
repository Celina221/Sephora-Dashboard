# Sephora-Dashboard
An exploratory analysis of 5,000+ Sephora products examining the relationship between price, exclusivity, and customer ratings. Built a multi-page Power BI dashboard revealing that non-exclusive products average higher prices than Sephora exclusives, and that exclusivity has minimal impact on customer ratings.

##Key Findings
-Non-exclusive products average higher prices than the exclusive products.
-Exclusivity had minimal impact on the customers rating of the product.
-Outlier product regarding price range shows that high price does not correlate with customer satisfaction.

##Tools Used
MySQL, PowerBI, Python (Data cleaning)
